Biography

I am a PhD candidate at Tsinghua University, working under the guidance of Professor Xin Jin in the Future Media Lab. Currently, I am also a visiting PhD student at Princeton University, where I collaborate with Professor Felix Heide in the Princeton Computational Imaging Lab.

My research lies at the intersection of optics, image processing, optimization and machine learning. I focus on advancing next-generation computational imaging, with a particular interest in scattering imaging, non-line-of-sight (NLOS) imaging, time-of-flight (ToF) based imaging and rendering. These technologies have transformative potential in fields such as autonomous vehicles, remote sensing, and medical diagnostics.

I am actively looking for a Postdoctoral Position starting in Spring 2025. If you are insterented in collaborating, I would love to connect!
