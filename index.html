Biography

I am a PhD candidate at Tsinghua University advised by Xin Jin in the Future Media Lab. Currently, I am also a visiting PhD student at Princeton University, under the guidance of Felix Heide in the Princeton Computational Imaging Lab.

My work is at the intersection of optics, image processing, optimization and machine learning. I have been working on the research of next-generation computational imaging for volumetric scattering imaging and non-line-of-sight (NLOS) imaging, which holds significant promise in applications such as autonomous vehicles, remote sensing, and medical diagnostics.
